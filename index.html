Tourist Places

Ambadevi Temple

One of Amravati's oldest temples, dedicated to Goddess Ambadevi. Famous for Navratri celebrations and divine architecture.

Bamboo Garden

India's first bamboo park, with 65+ bamboo species, toy train, cactus garden, and eco-sculptures..

Chatri Talao

Historical reservoir built in 1888, perfect for peaceful walks and scenic sunset views.

Wadali Talao

Popular lake for boating, family picnics, and photography. Includes garden, zoo, and peaceful sunset views.
